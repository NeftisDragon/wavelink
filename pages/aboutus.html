Brand Story

During the Great Depression, international trade fell by more than 50%, preventing major companies from exporting their products to different countries across the world. After the Second World War ended in 1945, the global economy started recovering and companies began mass-producing goods again, however, exportation was still a challenge given to the number of ships that sank during the war. In 1947, WaveLink was founded as small shipping company in NYC under the name of Wavefront Shipping Co., providing shipping services for companies such as Steinway & Sons and Yamaha Corporation. As time went by, the company grew and technology evolved, offering larger, faster, and different means of transport. Today we are the largest distributor of musical intruments and studio equipment. Companies choose WaveLink because we provide fast and secure shipping to more than 120 countries in 6 different continents.
